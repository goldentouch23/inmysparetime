Like I've stated before my interest in make up began at an early age and is still something I love today. Here you will find cosmetic tutorials, such as eyebrows and lashes. I hope you enjoy.

Eyebrow Tutorials

  1. Trace your eyebrow, try to follow the natural shaping (if you have one). Start at the bottom of your brow, by beginning to trace the front of the brow on back.
  2. Fil in the brows with light strokes.
  3. Start brushing the front of the brows on back.
  4. Use concealer to start shaping the bottom of your brow.
  5. Use foundation to begin shaping the top of your brow.
  6. Use blender/sponge to blend in the concealer 1st then the foundation.

Lash Tutorials

  1. Place your desired lash glue on the band of the lash. Wait about 30 seconds for glue to dry a little or becomes tacky.
  2. Place lash line in the center of the lash line.
  3. You can use your tweezers or finger to make sure both ends of the lash adhere to the lash line.
  4. Use the bottom end of your tweezers to secure lashes in place, making sure the natural lashes blend in.
  5. Repeat the same steps for the other eye.
